Chicago Student Rent Search

If you are looking for a place to leave while you are studing in Chicago, this is the site for you.

What is CSRS?

Is a brand new application that use data from the government to help you to get a place to live while you studing. Chicago can be a big city, but with this app you can get very close to the university in a cheaper way, so you can save money and keep your studies.
